RBI GUIDELINES VIOLATED:

As per RBI Circular No. RBI/2024-25/30 DoS.CO.PPG.SEC.1/11.01.005/2024-25 dated April 29, 2024 on "Fair Practices Code for Lenders - Charging of Interest", the RBI has expressly prohibited the following unfair practices:

a) "Charging of interest from the date of sanction of loan or date of execution of loan agreement and not from the date of actual disbursement of the funds to the customer."

b) "In the case of loans being disbursed by cheque, instances were observed where interest was charged from the date of the cheque whereas the cheque was handed over to the customer several days later."

c) "Collecting one or more instalments in advance but reckoning the full loan amount for charging interest."

The RBI has DIRECTED all Regulated Entities to:
- Refund the excess interest and other charges to customers immediately
- Review practices regarding mode of disbursal of loans and application of interest
- Take corrective action, including system level changes
